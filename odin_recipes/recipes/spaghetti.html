Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic pasta dish featuring spaghetti served with a rich, slow-cooked meat and tomato sauce.
Known for its hearty flavor and simplicity, it’s a timeless meal that can be easily adapted to different tastes.

Ingridients

  • pasta
  • ground meat (beef or beef & pork)
  • onion
  • garlic
  • tomato sauce
  • oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • herbs

Steps

  1. Boil salted water, add spaghetti pasta, and cook until al dente.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add chopped onion and cook until soft, then add garlic.
  3. Add the meat and fry until browned, breaking it into small pieces.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce or add canned tomatoes and mix well.
  5. Add salt, pepper, and herbs. A little sugar can be added if the sauce is too acidic.
  6. Cook on low heat for 15–30 minutes.
  7. Drain the pasta and mix with the sauce or serve the sauce on top.
  8. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese and enjoy!
