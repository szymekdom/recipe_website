Burger

Description

A timeless favorite made with a juicy, flame-grilled beef patty, layered with crisp lettuce, fresh tomato, and sliced onion.
Topped with melted cheese and our signature house sauce, all served on a soft, toasted bun.
Simple, satisfying, and full of bold flavor.

Ingridients

  • 1 egg
  • 500 grams of ground beef
  • tomato
  • lettuce
  • onion
  • cheddar cheese
  • Brioche rolls
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of mayo
  • 1 teaspoon of ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon of mustard

Steps

  1. Mix mayonnaise, ketchup, and mustard together to create a simple classic sauce.
  2. Form the ground beef into a patty slightly larger than the bun and season it with salt and black pepper.
  3. Cook the patty on a hot pan or grill for 3â€“4 minutes per side until browned and juicy.
  4. Place a slice of cheddar on top of the patty during the last minute of cooking and let it melt.
  5. Lightly toast the burger buns until golden and warm.
  6. Spread the sauce on the bottom bun, add fresh lettuce, place the cheesy patty on top, then layer with tomato, onion, and pickles before closing with the top bun.
  7. Serve the burger hot and enjoy.
